Our Heritage

A Century of
Engineering Excellence

Founded in 1939, CET stands as one of India's oldest engineering institutions — shaping generations of the brightest minds in science, technology, and innovation.

1939

Foundation

CET established as one of India's earliest engineering colleges under the Travancore State.

1956

Expansion

New departments of Electrical, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering added.

1972

Postgraduate Programs

National recognition and the launch of postgraduate research programs.

2000s

Research Era

Advanced research centers established, launching alumni into global institutions.
